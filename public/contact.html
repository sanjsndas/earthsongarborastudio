🌿 Before you write, wander with us...

The best conversations start with walking barefoot on our nursery paths. We host monthly “Brown & Green Open Soil Hours” — no appointment needed, just curiosity. Bring a jar of your own garden dirt and we’ll read its story. Also, our digital newsletter, "The Rhizosphere", shares deep seasonal guidance. Let us know if you'd like to receive woodland whispers.

📸 Instagram: @earthsong_arboria 🌱 Substack: Soil & Soul 🎧 Podcast: Beneath the Canopy

✨ Need urgent garden triage? Call our hotline between 10am–2pm ET.

🚗 Directions from nearby

From Hawkmeadow centre, follow Arbor Road east for 2.3 miles until you see the moss-covered stone sign. Turn onto the gravel lane lined with hazelnut hedges. Ample parking near the greenhouse. Public bus #12 stops at the valley turn, and we offer a bike rack made from reclaimed locust wood.

🌾 Studio Apprenticeships

We accept two seasonal interns each spring — hands-on education in permaculture, soil microscopy, and plant propagation. Send a short letter of interest and your relationship with the land to learn@earthsongarboria.com. Brown hands and green hearts welcome.
