🍪 Cookies Policy — Digital Seeds & Consent

We use only the lightest sprinkling of cookies — like dusting compost over a bed — never for surveillance, only for harmony and essential function.

🌾 Last updated: May 16, 2026 | Version 2.0 — Minimal & Mindful

Just as a garden grows best with minimal intervention, our website thrives on trust. At Earthsong Arboria Studio, we respect your autonomy. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, which ones we deploy (very few), and how you can manage them. We never use tracking cookies for advertising or cross-site profiling. Your visit here is like a quiet walk through the nursery — unobserved and unhurried.

🍃 Our core promise: No creepy trackers, no analytics that follow you across the web. All cookies we use are either strictly necessary or functional, such as remembering if you’ve dismissed a notification. You are always in control.

🌿 1. What are cookies? (tiny seed data)

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, phone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences (like language, display settings, or login status) over a period of time, so you don’t have to re-enter them each time. Think of them as little biodegradable markers — but unlike most sites, ours are very shy and respect your boundaries.

📋 2. Our cookie catalog — transparent as creek water

We use only essential and functional cookies. None are used for marketing, behavioral profiling, or selling data. Below is the complete list of cookies you may encounter on our domain:

⚙️ 3. Your cookie stewardship (manage & revoke)

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies altogether. However, blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website (for instance, the cookie consent banner might appear repeatedly). But don’t worry — our core content and forms will still work.

🍪 Manage your cookie preferences right here — this panel allows you to opt in/out of functional cookies that are not strictly essential (currently only the optional "gardener_reminder" cookie). Essential cookies cannot be disabled because they ensure basic site operations.

Additionally, you can configure your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) to block all cookies or notify you when a cookie is set. Please consult your browser's help section for detailed instructions.

🔗 4. External embeds & resources (very minimal)

Our site may occasionally embed content from Vimeo (for workshop recordings) or Calendly (for appointment booking). These third parties may set their own cookies, but only when you actively interact with the embedded widget (e.g., pressing 'play' on a video or loading a scheduling widget). We do not load any external scripts by default unless you give explicit interaction. However, we recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies: Vimeo's cookie policy and Calendly's cookie policy.

🌸 Our commitment: We choose embedded tools that prioritize data minimization. No external fonts from Google (we self-host) and no social media share buttons that track you. Our digital footprint is as light as a hummingbird’s.

📊 5. Privacy-first analytics (no personal data)

We care about understanding which of our articles on native plants or soil regeneration are most helpful. However, we do not use Google Analytics or any invasive tracking. Instead we use a minimal, self-hosted analytics tool (Plausible / Fathom-like approach) that aggregates only basic counts: page views, referrer domains (e.g., “from a search engine”), and rough region (country level). No IP addresses are stored, no user‑specific identifiers, no cross-site tracking. You can learn more about our commitment to digital sovereignty on our Privacy Policy page.

Because these analytics do not involve any personal data or unique identifiers, they do not require cookie consent under GDPR/CCPA — but we tell you anyway because transparency is our root value.

📜 6. Your consent & legal harmony

When you first visit our website, we display a minimal cookie banner informing you about our cookie use. By continuing to use our site or by clicking “Accept” on the banner, you consent to our use of functional and essential cookies as described. You may withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting the preference panel above or clearing cookies via your browser. Our legal basis for essential cookies is legitimate interest (website function), while for optional functional cookies, we rely on your explicit consent.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the UK, or California, you have specific rights concerning automated decision-making and data portability — none of which we perform. Still, our policy aligns with GDPR, ePrivacy Directive, and CCPA standards.

🧒 7. Children’s privacy & cookies

Our site does not target children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. The minimal cookies we set are not used to build profiles or track behavior across sessions. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about cookie storage regarding a minor, please use the browser tools to delete cookies or contact us.

🍂 8. Growing with transparency: policy updates

We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Any material changes will be notified via our website notice or email (if we have your address). The “last updated” date at the top indicates the latest revision. By continuing to use our site after any modifications, you accept the revised policy. We encourage you to review it periodically — just as you would check your compost heap for health.

✉️ 9. Questions? Talk to the Digital Steward

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, your preferences, or if you’d like to request a copy of the data stored in any cookie (though we store no personal data there), please reach out — we're happy to help.
